Turkey Club Sandwich

Description

Succulent chicken plus ultra-savory, crispy skin means you have a guaranteed game day hit on your hands. These oven-crisped Garlic-Parmesan Wings offer incredible flavor without the hassle of deep-frying. Coated in garlic and Parmesan cheese, they’re a mouthwatering alternative to a traditional, sauced Buffalo wing. For a chicken Parmesan inspired appetizer, serve these golden wings with a bowl of marinara for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon table salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon black pepper, divided
  • 1/2 cup (4 oz.) unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced (about 1 1/2 tbsp.)
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 4 oz parmesan cheese, grated (about 1 cup), divided

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Set a wire rack inside 2 rimmed baking sheets.
  2. Toss together wings, oil, salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of the black pepper in a large bowl to coat. Divide wings between baking sheets, and place in a single layer. Bake in preheated oven until browned and crispy, about 50 minutes, turning halfway through.
  3. When wings are almost finished cooking, heat butter, garlic, crushed red pepper, and remaining 1/4 teaspoon black pepper in a small saucepan over medium-low until fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove from heat, and stir in 3/4 cup of the Parmesan.
  4. Remove wings from oven, and immediately transfer to a large bowl. Add garlic butter mixture; toss to coat. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan.
